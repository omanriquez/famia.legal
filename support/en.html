Frequently Asked Questions

How does iCloud sync work?

Famia uses iCloud to automatically sync your data across all your Apple devices. Make sure iCloud is enabled on your device and you're signed in with your Apple ID.

How many members can I add?

The free version of Famia allows you to add up to 3 family members. With Famia Pro, you can add unlimited members.

How do I enable Face ID/Touch ID?

Go to Settings in the app and enable "Biometric Authentication". This will protect your family information with Face ID or Touch ID.

Can I export my data?

Yes, you can export all your data from the Settings section. Data is exported in PDF format.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. All your data is stored only on your device and in your personal iCloud. Famia does not have access to your information and we don't share it with third parties.

How does the Famia Pro free trial work?

Famia Pro offers a 7-day free trial. During this period, you'll have full access to all premium features. You can cancel anytime from your Apple ID account.

Can I use Famia on iPad and iPhone?

Yes, Famia is optimized for iPhone, iPad, and Mac (Catalyst). Your data syncs automatically across all your devices.
